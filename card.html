Cobra Snake

Write an interesting fact about your favorite animal...

  • Scientific Name: Naja
  • Average Length: between 6 feet and 10 feet long
  • Average Lifespan: 20 years
  • Habitat: forests, and heavily wooded areas

Naja species vary in length and most are relatively slender-bodied snakes. All have a characteristic ability to raise the front quarters of their bodies off the ground and flatten their necks to appear larger to a potential predator.
